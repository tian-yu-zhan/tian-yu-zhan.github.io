Tianyu is the Director of Strategic Capability at [AbbVie](https://www.abbvie.com/). He works closely with the executive leadership of Data and Statistical Sciences (DSS) to ensure governance strategies are aligned. Tianyu has delivered profound business impact at AbbVie, and was honored with three President Awards and one Development Award. As a dedicated mentor and strategic thinker, he leads critical company-wide and department-wide initiatives to elevate scientific standards. He has published 25+ peer-reviewed articles (including 15+ statistical articles), two book chapters, and one monograph on deep-learning-assisted statistical methods. Tianyu also serves as an Associate Editor for three statistical journals and has conducted over 125 peer reviews for the scientific community.
